About the System

This system predicts student performance based on various factors such as attendance, previous grades, study hours, assignment scores, and participation in extracurricular activities.

By uploading a CSV file with the necessary data, the system will use our trained model to predict the final grade and categorize the performance of students.

The predicted final grade will be displayed along with visualizations showing the distribution of grades and performance categories.

The goal of this system is to provide insights into student performance, helping educators and students to identify areas of improvement and achieve better academic outcomes.
